What We Offer

Touch The Light

Value based classes are given in schools to students of class 6, 7 and 8.

Positive Change Course

A course to learn how to harness your thoughts, emotions and responses of your daily life by use (using) your meditation skills, making time & space for meditation to establish a daily practice.

Positive Thinking Course

For those who are not sure if they are ready for meditation but would like to understand the workings of the mind better, gain greater clarity and learn how to manage their thoughts.

Stress Free Living Course

Stress has been identified as a leading cause of many health problems. Largely psychological, rather than physical it affects nearly all of us at some point in our lives.

Special Talks (Every 2nd & 4th Saturday)

A collection of talks and speeches from a variety of different fields are delivered for a positive outlook.

Workshops

In addition to Raja Yoga meditation courses, Brahma Kumaris run workshops on personal development.

Counselling

Spiritual conselling is provided to persons in need.

Women Empowerment Program

Women are encouraged to highlight problems and issues relating to women. And they discuss and debate all issues thoroughly.

BRAHMA KUMARIS SANGAM TIRTHDHAM


Brahma Kumaris is the largest worldwide spiritual movement led by women and dedicated to personal transformation and world renewal.
Founded in 1937, Brahma Kumaris has spread on all continents and has had an extensive impact in many sectors as an international NGO.
Our real commitment is to help individuals transform their perspective of the world from material to spiritual.
It supports the cultivation of a deep collective consciousness of peace and of the individual dignity of each soul.
